SILAWAK - Sistem Informasi Layanan Wakaf

Project Overview

SILAWAK is a digital information system designed to streamline the registration and management of wakaf (endowment) lands in Kabupaten Sukoharjo. The platform enables citizens to easily access information, submit applications, and monitor the status of their wakaf-related services transparently and efficiently.

Project Information

  • Category: Web Application
  • Client: Kantor Pertanahan Kabupaten Sukoharjo
  • Date: 2025
  • Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
